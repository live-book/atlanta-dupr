# About

The Atlanta DUPR Pickleball Club consists of Pickleball players who are interested in making measurable progress. We mainly play games and submit the scores to DUPR in order to get quantitative ratings.

# Why Use DUPR?

Dynamic Universal Pickleball Rating (DUPR)open in new window enables players to get ratings by submitting scores of any games to UDPR; unlike other rating systems that only provide ratings for tournament scores.

# Pickleball Rating

Types of Pickleball ratings

  • assessment-based rating: self-rating and coach-rating
  • score-based rating: UTPR and DUPR

# Assessment-Based Rating

Assessment-based rating refers to subjective rating assigned to a player based on skill rating defined by established organization(s), such as USA Pickleball (USAPAopen in new window).

  • Self-rating refers to players assign ratings to themselves based on predefined skill rating definition.
  • Coach-rating refers to players assign ratings by a Pickleball coach the coach's knowledge and predefined skill rating definition.

# Score-Based Rating

A score-based rating refers to quantitative ratings assigned to players based on their game scores. Two major organizations provide score-based ratings: USA Pickleball Tournament Player Rating (UTPR)open in new window and Dynamic Universal Pickleball Rating (DUPR)open in new window.

  • UTPR provides ratings for players who play in UTPR affiliated tournaments.
  • DUPR provides ratings for players who play in DUPRopen in new window and PPAopen in new window affiliated tournaments, as well as players who submit their scores of any game to DUPR.
